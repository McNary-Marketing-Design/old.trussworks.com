Trussworks Double Deck display system - 20 x 60 Gilna Rail

The Gilna Rail design offers a central lounge are and casual spaces on either side. It has over 550 sq. ft. of deck space  with 2 straight stairways and landings. Downstairs, the stairways are funneled to a center controlled entry space. 2 large counters are centered on the left and right side for meeting and evaluating new prospects. Monitors and large graphics tell the tale on the backwall while demos are handled on the front 2 center legs with column mount workstations. And literature is dispensed off the end columns. This will fit in a 50’ wide space.
