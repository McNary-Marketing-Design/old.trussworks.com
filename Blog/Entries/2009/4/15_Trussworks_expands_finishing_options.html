Trussworks expands finishing options

 

Hi Guys and Girls,


Well, we have gotten away with offering only 6 standard powder coat colors for a long, long time. We have decided it's time to step up to the plate and stock a few more colors. But not just a few more.. we have DOUBLED our standard colors. From 6 to 12, all without upcharges or extended lead times. Many of our new colors are semi-metallic for that high-tech look and feel.


Since we were expanding powder coat colors and we have a few laminate products on the drawing board, we figured we may as well step up there as well. So.. once again, we DOUBLED our standard laminate offerings, stocking 8 instead of 4 Wilsonart laminates. We also have a Nevamar board and although the laminates cost more, we just couldn't resist offering some of their awesome finishes so, we have 6. It's a 10% upcharge on the item, but we think it's so worth it.


Anyway, check out all of our finishes at http://www.trussworks.com/Color_options.html We hope you like them as much as we do.


Thanks and Happy Wednesday!


Roy


PS.. Watch for our new laminate products to show up in the coming weeks. We think you'll be very excited.
