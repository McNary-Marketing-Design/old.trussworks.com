Hollywood, 20 x 20, 20 x 30 , 20 x 40 - 1 booth, 3 sizes

 

The Hollywood was designed with 2 goals in mind. Make a statement with huge graphics and reconfigure simply using everything! To do this we created 2 similar structures that crisscross each other in a 20 x 20 or 20 x 30 space but sit next to each other in larger spaces. We also decided to show an option letting the truss frame the mega-graphics which saves quite a bit of graphic cost.


Attached find 5 different configurations of the Hollywood. 2 each 20 x 20s, 1 with full graphics, the other with truss framing it. The other 3 are a 20 x 30 and 2 - 20 x 40 configurations. Also consider just using one structure at about half the cost!


Retail for the hardware with 16 lights and 3 tubs - $18,410

Graphics @ $22 / sq. ft.

Beam graphics assuming covering both sides of all 4 beams. - $3,572

Full panel graphics (8 total)  - $14,884
