Happy holidays! a 10 x 10 a 20 x 20  & 6 new workstations

 

Hi everyone,


I know many of you are off this week but the busy little elves at Trussworks are hard at it.  The Entasi-shaped Hillcrest generated so much interest we created a 10 x 10 and 20 x 20 to shrink to or grow into. The workstations are all on one sheet with pricing.


The Coronado is another value priced 20 x 20, the hardware including the tower, the oval designer counter, 2 Davis workstations, 8 of our new telescopic lights and cases retails for $15,174. Graphics assuming $22 / sq. ft. would retail for $5,544.00.


The Irvine is basically the center 10' of the Hillcrest. It makes for a completely unique 10' display. While far from our least expensive 10 x 10, we feel it is still a great value retailing for $5,590 including lights and 3 cases. Graphics at $22 / sq. ft. would retail for $1,716.00. The details for this one are already on our site. http://www.trussworks.com/EZ6_Irvine.html
