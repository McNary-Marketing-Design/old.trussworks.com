EZ12 kits are complete and up!

 

Hello all,


Trussworks is proud to announce that we have completely revamped our EZ12 collapsible truss  kit offerings. We redesigned and updated our existing 8 kits and we added 7 more for a total of 15!

Check them out at http://www.trussworks.com/EZ12_Kits.html


Each of these kits is complete with a large high res image, a plan view, an isometric with graphic dimensions, and a color coded exploded view for assembly.


While all of the kits are priced out in retail, 3 of the kits are priced out in 3 different heights. We are very excited about these kits and hope that all this info helps make your job easier!


Thanks for looking!
