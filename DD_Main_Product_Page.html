Trussworks Double Deck Exhibit system

Mix and match decks, stairs and bridges..

        Add infills, graphics and accessories to make it yours!

Trussworks double decks are pre-engineered to meet most convention center requirements and assemble fast. While other sizes and shapes are available, we pre-engineered the most common sizes to give you a quick out of the box solution.


When using our double decks, you start by choosing a deck size and  stair configuration to go with it. Keep in mind in most cities, a deck with one stairway has a max occupancy of 9 so larger decks should generally have more stairways.
