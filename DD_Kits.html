Trussworks Double Deck Exhibit system

Whether you have a 20 x 20 or a 60 x 100, Trussworks Double Decks can be configured to meet your needs. We have pre-designed a number of kits for the most popular display sizes but there is no limitation to what you can build. Some of our larger kits can be broken down to create a smaller kit. 


You will see a number of our accessories such as shelving or A/V mounts in the kits. These are not part of the kits but are there in an attempt to help you visualize ways to use your display. We truly hope these ideas help you create the best display for you.
