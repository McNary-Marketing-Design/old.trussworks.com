Hyperlite tool-less hybrid truss / extrusion display system


Lots of graphics with minimal structure


            Hyperlite marries the strength of truss to the elegance of extrusion!

Hyperlite is an ultimately reconfigurable system. As you increase your display size or change your design, you will typically be able to use all or most of your existing components. This makes Hyperlite an excellent display investment.


When designing with Hyperlite, you not only create connecting spans between elements but you create panel opportunities. Through good planning, you can make the most of your budget and your display by an efficient and effective use of graphics, solid panels and maybe even slatwall!
